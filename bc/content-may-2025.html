About the Boot Camp

The May 2025 Boot Camp is designed to prepare students for success in programming courses and provide professional development opportunities.

Purpose

  • Prepare Students for Success: Build foundational skills and confidence for programming courses.
  • Support Programming Students: Refresh knowledge for those with prior programming experience.
  • Build a Community: Foster connections with peers, mentors, and faculty.
  • Professional Development: Guest speakers, field trips, and networking opportunities.
