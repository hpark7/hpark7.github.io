EMPOWERING WOMEN

IN TECHNOLOGY


Women in Technology Java Programming Boot Camp will be held in-person from:
Friday, January 3, 2025 - Saturday, January 4, 2025.

The purpose of this boot camp is to:

  • Prepare students to succeed in Java Programming courses
    • Group for students who have been exposed to Java and want a refresher to prepare for other IT classes
  • Enable women in IT to form a community of support with peers, student mentors, and IT faculty
  • Provide professional development opportunities such as guest speakers and off-campus field trips

Registration Fee for non-GGC students: $25.00. Students who complete the program will receive a certificate and prize. Payment details will be emailed upon acceptance into the program. We may offer a fee waiver for non-GGC students with a GPA of 3.3 or higher. Please note that eligibility for this waiver will be reviewed on a case-by-case basis and is subject to available resources.


The Women in Technology (WIT) Java Programming Boot Camp is designed to address the significant underrepresentation of women in the field of technology. This focus aligns with the purpose of the grant funding the program, which is to provide support and opportunities for women pursuing careers in IT.

Participation in the boot camp is open to all students.We encourage anyone interested in enhancing their programming skills to apply.
