Programming Boot Camp 2025


Programming Boot Camp will be held in-person from - Wednesday, May 7, 2025 - Friday, May 9, 2025.

The purpose of this boot camp is to:

  • Prepare Students for Success in Programming Courses: Equip students with the foundational skills and confidence needed to excel in programming courses.
  • Support Group for Programming Students: Designed for students with prior exposure to any programming language who are seeking a refresher to prepare for advanced IT classes.
  • Build a Strong IT Community: Foster a supportive environment where students can connect with peers, student mentors, and IT faculty to collaborate and grow together.
  • Offer Professional Development Opportunities: Provide enriching experiences such as guest speaker sessions, off-campus field trips, and networking opportunities to enhance career readiness.
