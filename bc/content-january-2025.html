About the Boot Camp

The January 2025 Boot Camp is an immersive, in-person experience designed to prepare students for success in programming courses and beyond.

Purpose

  • Prepare for Success: Build foundational skills for programming courses.
  • Support Programming Students: Refresh knowledge for students with prior exposure to programming.
  • Community Building: Connect with peers, mentors, and IT faculty for collaboration.
  • Professional Development: Gain insights through guest speakers, field trips, and networking opportunities.
