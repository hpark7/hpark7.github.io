January 2025 Boot Camp

Programming Boot Camp will be held in-person from Friday, January 3, 2025 - Saturday, January 4, 2025.

Application/Registration-Closed

Registration for non-GGC students includes a $25 fee. Fee waivers may be available for eligible non-GGC students based on academic merit (GPA of 3.3 or higher).

Purpose

  • Prepare students to succeed in Java Programming courses.
  • Designed for students who have been exposed to Java and want a refresher to prepare for other IT classes.
  • Enable students in IT to form a community of support with peers, student mentors, and IT faculty.
  • Provide professional development opportunities such as guest speakers and off-campus field trips.
