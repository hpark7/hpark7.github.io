May 2025 Boot Camp

Application/Registration

Programming Boot Camp will be held in-person from Wednesday, May 7, 2025 - Friday, May 9, 2025.

The purpose of this boot camp is to:

  • Prepare Students for Success in Programming Courses: Equip students with foundational skills to excel.
  • Build a Strong IT Community: Foster connections between students, mentors, and faculty.
  • Offer Professional Development: Guest speakers, field trips, and networking opportunities.
