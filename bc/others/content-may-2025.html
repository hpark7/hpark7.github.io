About the Boot Camp

The May 2025 Boot Camp is an in-person event designed to prepare students for programming success, build a strong IT community, and provide professional development opportunities.

Purpose

  • Prepare Students for Success: Build foundational programming skills and confidence.
  • Support Programming Students: Refresh knowledge for students with prior programming experience.
  • Build a Community: Foster connections with peers, mentors, and faculty.
  • Professional Development: Gain insights through guest speakers, field trips, and networking opportunities.
