Introduction

I teach and conduct research at the intersection of computing education, trust and safety, and responsible AI. My work focuses on inclusive, collaborative learning environments that foster critical digital literacy and problem-solving.

Courses I Teach

  • Advanced Programming – Algorithms (ITEC 3150)
  • Intermediate Programming – OOP and Data Structures (ITEC 2150)
  • Programming Fundamentals (ITEC 2140)

Project and Research Areas

  • CS Unplugged & Inclusive Pedagogy: Designing collaborative, active learning activities aligned with UDL and TILT principles.
  • AI Literacy and Digital Safety: Exploring how students understand and interact with intelligent systems, scams, and misinformation.
  • Trust & Safety in Computing Education: Integrating ethical reasoning and harm mitigation into programming and algorithm instruction.
  • Assessment & Learning Analytics: Analyzing student learning trajectories using state modeling and data-driven methods.
